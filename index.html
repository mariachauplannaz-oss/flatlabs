The problem

This is where your time gets wasted.

The reality

You designed the collection. You sourced the fabric. You're ready to produce. And now? You're stuck dragging anchor points for three hours to draw a t-shirt that already exists in a thousand variations on the internet.

The pain point

Free templates are unusable. Ungrouped. Misproportioned. Fragile. They aren't built for production — they're built to frustrate you.

The gap

You shouldn't have to choose between "free templates that don't work" and "enterprise software priced like a luxury car." FlatLabs fills the gap with technically correct flats for the 80% of cases where you need a clean, professional drawing — fast. Need full custom construction or a unique silhouette? That still belongs with your patternmaker. This is for everything else. There's something broken about that. We're fixing it. Shipping in July 2026.

The professional alternative? Real measurements, proper construction, full kit. €3,000 — minimum. More if you want the whole suite.

FlatLabs is the tool that should have existed between those two extremes. Now it does.
